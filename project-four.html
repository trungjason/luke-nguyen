DOWNTOWN MARKET

WEB&APP DESIGN

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Downtown Flea Market is a fictitious organization. Its main service is to host occasional events or flea markets where people can not only go shopping, but also relax, and enjoy a lot of exciting side activities.

The primary purpose of this project is to create an interactive prototyping system for DFW website, including 5 main pages: Homepage, About, Services/Ticket Pricing, Contact and FAQ page.

Based on user research, We identified a primary group of DFM's target audience and a user persona to understand their goals/interests, their motivations and all paint points/concerns they may have while browsing content or booking tickets on a website having the same services as DFM. We realized that the Services/Ticket Pricing is a very important page for users as they mostly stay longer on this page to go through all highlighted information about the event. When it comes to purchasing stage, they really want to process their payment quickly with clear and prompt confirmation. A smooth, simple payment process will win over user experience. This finding helped us focus during the design process giving priority to the Services/Ticket Pricing pate and Payment process.

We also analyzed some competitors such as Eastside Fle, Vancouver Flea Market, Vintage Qoo,‚Ä¶ to see how their websites look. Our most valuable finding showed that all competitor's websites are using the same traditional and vintage approach for their website design. Therefore, there will be a chance to make our DFM's website stand out by going with a modern and vivid creative design approach.

‚úçüèª Styling & sitemap

After the brainstorming process, 2 different branding concepts attached logo and brand title were developed. The 1st concept went with a trendy, bold, vivid, and colourful theme verse with the 2nd one going with a classic, vintage, and cozy tone. Based on user analysis, the 1st concept was selected to develop detailed UI components for our website.

Then, We developed a sitemap and concentrated on user flows in order to make our design more user-centered and, therefore, more successful from the user's point of view. We designed detailed user flows for Payment Process to make sure it will be optimal solution for user experience when user take steps to purchase tickets on site and make their payment effortlessly. User flow is very crucial as it helped us to visualize the number of steps a user takes to complete an expected task of goal and gave an idea of how to map out the screens when we develop wireframe.

Once sitemap is clear with all required information and the UI design system is ready, a low-fidelity wireframe is designed to have a view of how our website will be structured for design optimization. Next, We started to develop high-fidelity wireframe. To provide a cohesive brand image and user experience, the design theme for this website had to be inline and consistent with the Brand style tile, which is modern, vivid and clean yet intuitive enough to provide smooth user flow and an easy-to-understand interface. All contents and images are selected carefully and put together accordingly as final design.
