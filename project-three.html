PROJECT OVERVIEW

Saffronic offers high-quality saffron products accessible and affordable to everyone, who wants to strengthen their immune system and pursue a healthy lifestyle.

The main goal of this project is to design a brand-new website for Saffornic, including 4 main pages: Homepage, Shop page, About page, and Blog Page by using Wix.
